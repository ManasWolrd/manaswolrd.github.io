SteepFlanger

Flanger

SteepFlanger is a flanger effect plugin based on a hybrid FIR/IIR architecture, also supporting the classic Barberpole (infinite ramp) flanging mode.

Features
  • FIR Flanging — Classic flanging based on Finite Impulse Response filters, producing smooth, stable comb filter sweeps
  • IIR Flanging — Infinite Impulse Response flanging with steeper notches than FIR, but with high-frequency chirps when modulating delay time
  • Barberpole Flanging — Infinite ramp mode creating a continuously ascending or descending spiral sweep
